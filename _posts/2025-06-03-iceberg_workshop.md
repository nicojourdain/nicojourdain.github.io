On 3-4 June 2025, Martin Vancoppenolle and I are organising an international 2-day workshop on icebergs and sea ice at Sorbonne Université, Paris, France. The aim is to summarize recent work, identify gaps and outline perspectives on iceberg-sea ice interactions and their implications on the Southern Ocean

**List of participants:** Thierry Fichefet, Alexander Fraser, François Massonnet, Pierre Mathiot, Anna Olive Abello, Dan Atwater, Sarah Berthet, Clément Billy, Alex Brearley, Jacquemine Delfieu, Eva Lemaire, Yavor Kostov, Juliana Marson, Carolin Mehlmann, Joël Petit, Noé Pirlet, Pat Wongpan, Clara Burgard, Casimir de Lavergne, Katherine Hutchinson, Gurvan Madec, Matthias Noël, Élise Ortega, Evéa Piedagnel, Pierre Rampal, Jeff Ridley, Clément Rousset, Jean-Baptiste Sallée, Max Thomas.
